Properties We Service

Multi-Family Existing Stock

  • We’ve got the experience to support your existing property.

  • Identify cost saving measures using Mass Saves and emerging technologies that reduce your utility expenses

  • Steam traps? Rotting plumbing? Clay sewer laterals? We know how to cost effectively repair or replace.

  • Troublesome tenants? We know how to reestablish the tenant-landlord relationship without going to court.

  • No Capital Project too big. We have the team and will manage the entire process regardless of the scope or scale.

Multi-Family Existing Stock

  • Pre-construction plan review: Our leasing team talks with tenants every day. We will help you identify what your future tenants are looking for before you build it so you don’t have expensive change orders mid-construction.

  • Fill Vacancy Fast! We can develop virtual walkthroughs and photo realistic renderings from construction plans so agents can rent apartments while you focus on building

  • Post-Construction repairs: We get you focused on your next project by working with your contractors for post construction repairs under warranty.

  • Establish Rent Roll: We’ve done this before. We will establish your rent roll so you can capitalize on your asset.
